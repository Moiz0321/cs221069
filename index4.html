    About Us:
    About Us Chase Value is a leading department store retailer in Pakistan, which has been in business since 2009. It is a one-of-its-kind retail store where all middlemen have been eliminated from the supply chain to minimize the product cost and transfer all the benefits of the resulting savings to customers in the form of reduced prices.
    With a promise of wholesale prices all year round, Chase Value owns three branches in Karachi, one in Multan, one in Bahawalpur, one in Faisalabad & one in Islamabad, and an e-commerce website that serves all across Pakistan.
